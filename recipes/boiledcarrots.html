Boiled Carrots

The correct way to boil carrots.

Description

I've been boiling my carrots like this for centuries, thought I'd share my recipe.

Ingredients

  • Carrots
  • Washer Machine
  • Soap

Steps

  1. Throw in your organic carrots.
  2. Fill your washer machine with Soap.
  3. Boil for 60mins.
  4. Hand dry carrots.
  5. Enjoy!
